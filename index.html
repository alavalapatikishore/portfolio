About

I'am Alavalapati Poorna Satya Hara Sai Kishore

I started my professional journey as a JavaScript Developer in 2017, where I honed my skills in scripting and application development while gaining exposure to ServiceNow development. In 2019, I transitioned to the National Highways Authority of India (NHAI), a central government organization, serving as a consultant until 2022. During this tenure, I played a key role in developing and optimizing ServiceNow-based solutions to streamline data management and improve collaboration among consultants, road companies, NHAI headquarters, and Project Director offices in Andhra Pradesh.

Since 2022, I have been working as a ServiceNow Developer in a client-facing(VOIP) capacity. My expertise spans IT Service Management (ITSM), integrations, HTML, CSS, portal page design, JavaScript, virtual agents, and workflow automation. With a proven track record of delivering efficient and scalable solutions, I am committed to leveraging my technical expertise to drive innovation and operational excellence.
